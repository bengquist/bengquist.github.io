About Me

Passion for Code

When I was in 7th grade my family was given our first computer, ever since then I have been amazed how everything technology works. I became fascinated and was glued to the computer screen trying to learn as much as I could. I built my first computer when I was 17 with the money I saved up from my first job at a grocery store. After a weekend of troubleshooting, it finally booted up for the first time. This is one of the greatest feelings and its one I chase every single day. Creating, learning, and managing new technologies is a huge hobby for me. I love creating a product for people that has actual value and solves problems. There is not a day that goes by where I am not doing some form of coding. Whether it's watching random tutorials on YouTube and Udemy or talking about it with the friends I've met while coding. Code is life.
