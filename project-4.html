Lit - Personal Project

Completion: July 2017
Role: Lead Developer

As a music enthusiast, I felt inspired to create this music-based social media application. With "lit", users can log in using their spotify credentials, search through new music, and share their playlists with other users.
Technologies used:
- ReactJS and Redux for the management of all front-end data and dynamic components
- Express framework in addition to Node.js for server-side functionality
- Database management: PostgreSQL and massive
- Additional libraries and APIs: Sotify API, Momentjs, and Semantic UI
